About Me

Creating Learning That Engages & Transforms

I'm passionate about designing learning experiences that are not only informative but also engaging, interactive, and aligned with business objectives. My approach combines pedagogical expertise with modern technology to create solutions that learners love.

I specialize in the full learning design lifecycle—from analysis and strategy to development, implementation, and evaluation. My work is grounded in evidence-based practices like ADDIE, SAM, and Design Thinking.

My Design Process

A structured approach to creating effective learning experiences

01

Discover & Analyze

Understanding business goals, learner needs, and technical constraints through stakeholder interviews and data analysis.

02

Design & Strategy

Creating learning objectives, content structure, interaction design, and visual mockups aligned with pedagogical principles.

03

Develop & Build

Implementing the design using appropriate tools and technologies, ensuring accessibility and cross-platform compatibility.

04

Deploy & Evaluate

Launching the solution, tracking performance metrics, and making data-driven improvements for optimal results.
